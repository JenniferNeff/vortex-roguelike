This is a roguelike where you can navigate a dungeon.

                                        yku
Use these keys to move in 8 directions: h l
                                        bjn

Fight monsters by walking into them.
Pick up items by walking onto them and typing "t".
Use them by typing "I", for Invoke.
Walk up and down stairs with "<" and ">".
Quit the game by typing "q".

For more help, read readme.txt.
